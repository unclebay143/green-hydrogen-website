Benefit Of Green Hydrogen:

Sustainability: It has minimal environmental impact since it emits no carbon dioxide during production and usage

Versatility: Green hydrogen can be used for various applications, including power generation, transportation (fuel for hydrogen fuel cell vehicles), and industrial processes

Energy Storage: It acts as an energy carrier, allowing for excess renewable energy to be stored and used when needed
